# Fuel Station Sales Dashboard 2025 – Summary Report

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive fuel station transaction dashboard and analysis workbook for 2025. The solution consolidates operational and financial transaction data into an interactive reporting environment, enabling monitoring of sales performance, profitability, customer activity, fuel distribution, and operational efficiency.

## Dataset Summary

* Total Transactions: **31,895**
* Total Revenue: **₦1.72 Billion**
* Total Gross Profit: **₦63.44 Million**
* Total Fuel Volume Sold: **2.11 Million Litres**
* Average Revenue per Transaction: **₦54,021**

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Performance

Revenue exceeded **₦1.72 billion** during the reporting period, with the strongest monthly performance recorded in **December (₦229.84M)**, followed by **October (₦156.18M)** and **November (₦153.22M)**.

### Fuel Type Analysis

| Fuel Type | Revenue Contribution |
| --------- | -------------------- |
| PMS       | ₦1.02B               |
| AGO       | ₦645.50M             |
| DPK       | ₦60.24M              |

PMS remains the primary revenue driver, contributing approximately **59% of total revenue**.

### Asset Category Performance

| Asset Type | Revenue  |
| ---------- | -------- |
| Truck      | ₦980.16M |
| Bus        | ₦422.00M |
| Car        | ₦208.83M |
| Generator  | ₦78.16M  |
| Motorcycle | ₦33.84M  |

Truck-related sales generated the highest revenue, accounting for more than half of total sales.

### Payment Method Distribution

Revenue is evenly distributed across payment channels:

* Cash: ₦578.13M
* Transfer: ₦576.00M
* POS: ₦568.86M

This balanced distribution indicates strong adoption of both digital and traditional payment methods.

### Operational Trends

* Weekday revenue: **₦1.45B**
* Weekend revenue: **₦274.84M**

Sales activity is significantly higher during weekdays, representing approximately **84% of total revenue**.

### Attendant Performance

Top-performing attendants by revenue generated:

1. Kemi – ₦293.49M
2. Bola – ₦289.40M
3. Emeka – ₦289.21M
4. Ibrahim – ₦286.33M
5. Tunde – ₦282.84M

Performance among attendants is relatively balanced, indicating consistent operational productivity.

## Dashboard Features

* Revenue and profit tracking
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Fuel type performance monitoring
* Asset category segmentation
* Payment method analysis
* Attendant performance evaluation
* Interactive pivot-based reporting

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides a centralized view of fuel station operations and financial performance throughout 2025. PMS sales, truck-related transactions, and weekday operations are the primary contributors to revenue generation. The solution enables stakeholders to identify trends, monitor profitability, and support data-driven business decisions.
